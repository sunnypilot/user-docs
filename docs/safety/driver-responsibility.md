sunnypilot is a **Level 2 Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS)**.

This means that while the system can assist with steering, acceleration, and braking, **you, as the driver, are expected to be fully attentive and ready to take over at all times.** It is not a self-driving system.

If the vehicle does not behave as expected, you must intervene immediately.

For official context on the levels of automation and what Level 2 entails, please review the NHTSA guidelines: [**NHTSA: Levels of Automation**](https://www.nhtsa.gov/sites/nhtsa.gov/files/2022-05/Level-of-Automation-052522-tag.pdf)

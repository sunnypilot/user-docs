# Ford Vehicle Settings

Information about sunnypilot behavior on Ford vehicles. These notes apply when a Ford vehicle is connected or selected.

**Location**: `Settings -> Vehicle`

---

## MADS Support

Ford vehicles support [MADS](../../features/steering/mads.md) using the TJA (Traffic Jam Assist) button on the steering wheel. Press the TJA button to toggle steering assistance independently of cruise control.

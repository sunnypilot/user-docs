To ensure the persistence of local file modifications on your comma device across reboots, it is necessary to disable the automatic software update feature. Follow these steps:

1. Go to **Settings > Developer** on your comma device and enable the **Show Advanced Controls** option by toggling the switch to **ON**.
2. Navigate to **Settings > Software** on your comma device and enable the **Disable Updates** option by toggling the switch to **ON**.
3. Proceed to **Settings > Device** and select **Reboot**.
4. After the device has restarted, any local changes made directly on the comma device will be preserved.

**Important:** Local modifications applied before completing steps 1 and 2 will not be retained.

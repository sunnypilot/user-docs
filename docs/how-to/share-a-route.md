> [!info] Topic scope
> This guide generally applies to **sunnypilot, openpilot and openpilot-based forks** that support uploading routes to **[comma Connect](https://connect.comma.ai)**.
>
> Sections that are **sunnypilot-specific** (such as who provides assistance or which email to share with) are explicitly noted.

> [!important] Supported routes (sunnypilot-specific)
> The sunnypilot team currently **only provides assistance with routes uploaded to [comma Connect](https://connect.comma.ai) from supported comma devices running sunnypilot**.
>
> Routes uploaded to other services or platforms are currently **not supported**.

To help investigate issues, you may be asked to **share a driving route** so logs can be reviewed.

## Step 1: Prepare the Route
***

> [!important]
>
> **This step is required for all sharing options.**

> [!info] Route preparation only
> This step **does not share anything** by itself.  
> It ensures the route logs exist and won’t be deleted.

> [!important] Raw logs required (`rlogs`)
> For route review, we specifically look for **raw logs (`rlogs`)**.
>
> All **rlogs associated with the route must be fully uploaded** in **[comma Connect](https://connect.comma.ai)**.  
> If any rlogs are missing or partially uploaded, the route cannot be properly reviewed.

<details><summary><b>Tap here to expand Route Preparation steps</b></summary>

### 1. Upload all raw logs
1. Go to **https://connect.comma.ai**
2. Select the route
3. Open **Files**

    ![image|333x500, 100%](upload://8CyTACVKduX72bTZRQT813IQwiX.jpeg)

4. Under **All logs**, click **“upload X logs”**

    ![image|333x500](upload://t07cO8ReiZL0IlAYkKaeVkewr28.png)

5. Open **View upload queue** and wait until all uploads finish

    ![image|333x500](upload://iNtfyoqr1vB2Rooa5bjtRnUvo2u.png)

### 2. Preserve the route
- Open **More info**

    ![image|333x500](upload://j3GWtUE0IpItWEqB0symmuSUxNO.jpeg)

- Toggle **Preserved** ON

    ![image|333x500](upload://dZgExApKprtFzQciteVwpJE4Mhd.png)

</details>

## Step 2: Choose How to Share Access
***

> [!important]
> This step determines **who can see what**.
>
> - **Public route** → only the selected route is visible  
> - **Device sharing** → all routes on the device are visible

### Option A: Share a Public Route (Recommended)
***

<details><summary><b>Tap here to expand Option A instructions</b></summary>

> [!warning] Public route visibility
> 
> Making a route **Public** means:
> - Anyone with the route ID can access **that route**
> - It may include GPS location, speed, and timestamps
>
> **Best practices (same guidance used by comma staff):**
> - Start and end at **public places** (parking lots, stores)
> - Avoid homes, workplaces, or sensitive locations
> - Only share routes you’re comfortable making public

#### 1. Make the route public
- Open **More info**

    ![image|333x500](upload://j3GWtUE0IpItWEqB0symmuSUxNO.jpeg)

- Toggle **Public access** ON

    ![image|333x500](upload://pBSnqs3vMmqkHtnjt6qzH9xwftu.png)

#### 2. Share the Route ID
- Open **More info**

    ![image|333x500](upload://j3GWtUE0IpItWEqB0symmuSUxNO.jpeg)

- Copy the **Route ID**

    ![image|333x500](upload://z2or5lEvhp1emUV5Bgb8ugkrcrw.png)

#### 3. Post the Route ID

Post the route ID to:
- Your forum discussion topic  
- Or wherever route access was requested

✅ Only this route is accessible  
✅ No device-level access is granted

</details>

### Option B: Private Device Sharing
***

<details><summary><b>Tap here to expand Option B instructions</b></summary>

> [!warning] Full device access
> Sharing your device grants access to **all routes on the device**, not just one.
>
> Use this option only if you are **not comfortable making routes public**.

#### 1. Share your device
1. Go to **https://connect.comma.ai**
2. Click the **gear icon** on your device

    ![image|333x500](upload://p0A8Iua4MKzekmCDh3OyLLYOlf0.png)

3. Rename your device

    📌 Please rename your device to:  
    **Year / Make / Model + Your forum username**  
    (e.g., *2025 Genesis GV80 Coupe – sunnyhaibin*)

    ![image|333x500](upload://z7zPFD9N6zxsVpZabNX7CYWLhnL.png)

4. Share by email:
   - **sunnypilot users:** enter **[u]support@sunnypilot.ai[/u]** to allow the sunnypilot team to review all routes
   - **other users:** enter **any email address** you trust and wish to grant device access to

    ![image|333x500](upload://qIqs8A2dG012V3et5fBRsEyQJ4F.png)

5. Confirm the share

    ![image|337x500](upload://5GRkKOoS0TjGdizSqyErpnQkper.png)

#### 2. Share the Route ID
1. Select the route
2. Open **More info**

    ![image|333x500](upload://j3GWtUE0IpItWEqB0symmuSUxNO.jpeg)

3. Copy the **Route ID**

    ![image|333x500](upload://z2or5lEvhp1emUV5Bgb8ugkrcrw.png)

#### 3. Post the Route ID

Post the route ID to:
- Your forum discussion topic  
- Or wherever route access was requested

</details>

## Notes & Limitations
***

> [!warning] Log availability
>  
> - All raw logs (`rlogs`) must be **fully uploaded** before they can be properly reviewed
> - Incomplete uploads cannot be effectively analyzed
> - Only routes visible in **[comma Connect](https://connect.comma.ai)** can be reviewed

If you’re unsure which option to use, please feel free to ask, help is provided on a best-effort, community basis.
